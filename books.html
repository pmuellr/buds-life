Maurice "Bud" Mueller, MD, PhD, wrote an autobiography titled "Bud's Life". It's available in several formats:

  • Paperback 6"x9" from Lulu
  • PDF format for 8.5"x 11" pages
  • EPUB format for eReaders except the Kindle
  • MOBI format for the Kindle
  • HTML format to read on the web

If you have an new-ish iPod Touch or iPhone, you can download the EPUB version and drop in your Books in iTunes, and read it with the iBooks application.

If you have an older iPod Touch or iPhone, you can read the EPUB with the Stanza application.

"Bud's Life" should be available on the Kindle Store for $0.99 by October 9 2010 or so.
